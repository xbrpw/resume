Philosophy

Substance over style, but never boring

All projects are unique, but some design principles are universal. I believe that distilling a brand down to its simplest form (from messaging to visual expression) is crucial to effectively communicate your message. From that foundation, more can be added to enhance the message, functionally and without pretense.
